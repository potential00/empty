WHAT IS AI?

AI or Artificial Intelligence, is all about creating computer systems that can perform tasks that would usually require human intelligence. It's like teaching computers to think and learn on their own. AI has come a long way, from voice assistants like Siri and Alexa to self-driving cars and even helping doctors diagnose diseases. It's pretty amazing how AI is shaping our world and making our lives easier.
